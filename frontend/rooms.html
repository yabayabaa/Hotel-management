    Our Rooms

    Rooms where you can feel free

    At Hotel Amrit, each room offers a unique blend of comfort and serenity. Designed with thoughtful attention to detail, our rooms provide a peaceful sanctuary with modern amenities and a touch of Nepalese tradition, ensuring a relaxing and memorable stay.

    Rooms

    Suite (140 sqm) offers an elegant and expansive living space, perfect for a luxurious stay. Featuring an interconnected room with a king-size bed and an option to add an extra bed upon request, this suite provides both comfort and flexibility.

    Enjoy breathtaking monastery views from the front and a serene garden view at the back. The suite includes a spacious hall with a comfortable sofa, creating a perfect setting for relaxation. The attached bathroom features a bathtub with high-quality toiletries, while amenities such as an ironing board, iron, hair dryer, and a Western-style toilet ensure a well-equipped and pleasant stay.
